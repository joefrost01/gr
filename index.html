A lot of GR ideas are illustrated by the panda, but why? We love pandas, their black and white facade, perfect for our preferred stencil art style, exudes simplicity yet lands an impactful punch - a spontaneous burst of intrigue in the mundane. Their thoughtful, expressive faces capture the message in a truly compelling way. Steering clear of typical corporate imagery, our panda-centric content strikes as bold and refreshing. The panda's association with conservation, tackling climate change and wildlife protection aligns perfectly with our core values, symbolising many things we hold dear.
